Pozole

Pozole is a traditional Mexican pork soup cooked with hominy and flavored with red guajillo chiles for a deep smoky flavor. It is serve with shredded cabbage, cilantro, radisehs, onion, and garnish with lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Seasoning
  • Tomato
  • Guajillo Chiles
  • Hominy
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Boil, then simmer pork with garlic and salt until meat is cooked through.
  2. Boil tomatoes and chiles together, drain.
  3. Blend tomato and chiles with salt, garlic, oregano, cumin, and water.
  4. Shred pork, then discard bones and garlic.
  5. Stir sauce and pork into the pot, then simmer until flavors have melded.
  6. Ladle pozole into bowls and finish each serving with toppings.
